    Learn more about licenses

    Why does my enterprise software license require an annual renewal?

    🔰

    All infrastructure licenses include 1 full year of automated engineering maintenance and core system patches to guarantee continued support and security.

    How do I modify or pause my automated renewal schedule?

    🔰

    Maintenance plans renew automatically until you choose to opt out. You can disable automated updates after your initial deployment through your account dashboard or by contacting support.

    What are the primary benefits of an enterprise deployment model?

    🔰

    Learn more about our scalable enterprise deployment offerings, custom SLA terms, and priority data pipelines in our documentation guide.

    What is included in the premium engineering support package?

    🔰

    All premium tier deployments come with a full year of active infrastructure monitoring, dedicated security patch rollouts, and priority access to our core engineering team.

    What is the core difference between cloud and on-premise deployment?

    🔰

    We offer both flexible cloud-based infrastructure and dedicated on-premise deployments to align with your corporate security policies. Cloud deployments include instant automated system upgrades and real-time scaling out of the box. On-premise infrastructure licenses grant you complete environment control and standalone longevity, but system maintenance, data backups, and security patch configurations must be managed directly by your internal engineering operations team.
